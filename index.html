Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the subscription? ▼
Your subscription includes access to all video classes from Nursery to Class 10, complete study materials in PDF format, interactive quizzes, progress tracking, and lifetime updates.
Is the subscription really lifetime? ▼
Yes! Your lifetime subscription means you get permanent access to all content with one-time payment of ₹299. You can access the platform anytime without any recurring charges.
Can I watch videos offline? ▼
Currently, videos are streamed online. However, all study materials (PDFs) can be downloaded for offline access. We're working on offline video support.
What if I have technical issues? ▼
We have a dedicated support team ready to help. Contact us via email at support@ajblearn.com or call our helpline. We typically respond within 24 hours.
Can I cancel my subscription? ▼
Since it's a lifetime subscription with one-time payment, there's no cancellation needed. However, if you want a refund within 30 days of purchase, we offer 100% money-back guarantee.
